about

This is the blog of the faircloth-lab, a scientific workgroup interested in evolutionary biology; phylogenomics; comparative genomics; and the relationships between genetic diversity, phenotypic variation, and environmental differences. We dig working with different critters and using the unique traits of each to help answer our questions.

The theme we use here is derived from one created by Yuya Saito, and the blog is powered by the jekyll templating engine.
